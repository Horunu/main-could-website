§01 · Trade questions

The honest
small print.

What's the minimum order?

One case — 24 cans — to open an account. Most studios settle into a case or two a fortnight once their members find them.

How does pricing work?

Trade is from £1.80 a can by the case, dropping on pallet volumes. You set the shelf price; most venues sell at £3.20–£3.80. Full price list comes with your sample tray.

Delivery & lead times?

Free across Greater London on standing orders, chilled and carbon-neutral. First order ships within 3 working days of account set-up; reorders arrive on your chosen cadence.

Payment terms?

First order is pro-forma; after that, Net 14 on approved accounts. Pay by card or bank transfer — your reorders are handled through your trade portal.

Outside London?

We ship nationwide via chilled courier at cost. Tell us your postcode in the form and we'll quote delivery with your price list.
