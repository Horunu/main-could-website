What's in the can

Three things,
in the right ratio.

Not an energy drink — a functional one. Just three things, in balance: ceremonial-grade Kagoshima matcha, 250 mg of L‑Theanine to keep you level, and 50 mg of caffeine — half a coffee's worth. Nothing else. If it's in the can, it's on the can.

01 — Amino acid 250mg

L‑Theanine

The amino acid that makes matcha feel unlike coffee. It smooths the caffeine curve into a long, level plateau — so the afternoon stays even.

Plateau
02 — From the leaf 50mg

Caffeine

About half a coffee. Drawn from the matcha leaf itself — no synthetic anhydrous, no guarana, no taurine. Enough to lift, never enough to jitter.

Lift
03 — First harvest Grade A

Ceremonial Matcha

Stone-ground Kagoshima from the first harvest — the leaf you can't get in a sachet. Lifted with 0.4% pressed yuzu. Tastes like a tea room.
