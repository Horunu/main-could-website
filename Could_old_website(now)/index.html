Our Possibilities

Sparkling

Curated Effervescence

Our sparkling base is built for pleasure first: lightly fizzy, ultra-refreshing, and genuinely addictive to sip. Think fine, delicate bubbles, a smooth rounded flavour, and a crisp clean finish that lets the matcha shine. Every can is crafted to feel vibrant and uplifting, with just the right level of sparkle to make it feel special without overpowering the taste. The result is a drink that feels bright, elegant, and ridiculously easy to reach for again and again.

Botanicals

lemon balm, bacopa, korean ginseng

This trio of active ingredients, combined with B vitamins and magnesium, uniquely interacts with how caffeine is absorbed by your body. Layered with L-theanine from green tea, it's designed for focus that feels clean, calm and clear – not wired. Lemon balm is traditionally used to take the edge off stressy days, while bacopa and Korean ginseng are known in nootropic and adaptogen circles for supporting mental performance and resilience. Together, they round off the sharper side of caffeine, so your functional drink feels more like a gentle on-switch than a jolt.

No added sugar

Kagoshima Matcha and Kito Yuzu fruits

Quality ingredients don't need added sugars. Shade-grown Uji matcha from Kyoto adds bright green depth, layered umami and a naturally sweet finish. Shading the tea plants before harvest protects their amino acids (like L-theanine), which keeps the flavour smooth and reduces bitterness, so the cup tastes indulgent without needing syrups or artificial tricks. Instead of spiking your drink with free sugars – the kind linked with weight gain, tooth decay and higher cardiometabolic risk when eaten in excess – we let the raw ingredients do the talking.
